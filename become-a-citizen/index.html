Citizenship

Become a citizen of Elmwycke if you live in south-east Britain.

What are the benefits to citizenship?

» Ability to join the College

» Ability to enlist in the Police Force

» Ability to become an ambassador

» Full access to state-run programs

» Ability to vote


What are the criteria to apply for citizenship?

» You must reside in south-east Britain (Greater London, Berkshire, Buckinghamshire, Oxfordshire, Hampshire, Surrey, Kent, Isle of Wight, East Sussex, West Sussex or Essex)

» You must be over the age of 13
