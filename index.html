Kingdom of Waterside Park

Waterside Park is a micronation located within Walton-on-Thames, SE England.

Founded in June 2015, Waterside Park's history starts with the Shaw Kingdom.

In 2019, the Republic of Fishmore, later known as the Baffin Republic was founded. In 2021, the Baffin Republic and Waterside Park merged.

We've now signed multiple treaties of mutual recognition, and we're a part of 3 seperate organisations.
