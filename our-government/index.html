The Royal College

The Royal College of Elmwycke is the legislative branch of the government.

Made up of 7 seats, 2 for every constituency and one for the Speaker of the Royal College, it represents the people of Elmwycke to the government.

The Prime Minister is whomever leads the party holding the most seats in the College, currently Jerry Qian of the Competent Fools' Party.

The constituencies are:

Waterside Park #1 (Jerry Qian)
Waterside Park #2 (Joe Seth)
Elmwycke Field North #1 (Liam Piper)
Elmwycke Field North #2 (Adam Wang)
Elmwycke Field South #1 (Calvin Harrington)
Elmwycke Field South #2 (Nayan Serpant)
Seat of the Speaker (Larry Martin)

The Speaker

The Speaker of the Royal College is the head of state and government.

The current Speaker of the Royal College is Larry Martin.
